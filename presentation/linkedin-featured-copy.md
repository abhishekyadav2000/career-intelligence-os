## Long (Featured section — full project write-up)

### Career Intelligence OS — Sponsor-Aware Enterprise Career Intelligence

**Problem:** Enterprise technology hiring in DFW spans 50+ sponsor-friendly employers with overlapping role families. Candidates lack a structured system to prioritize companies, score role fit, and iterate based on real conversations.

**What I Built:**
- Modular Python pipeline: CSV ingestion → schema validation → six-category role fit scoring → recommendation engine
- Streamlit dashboard with 9 tabs: overview, company ranking, role fit drill-down, sponsorship signals, networking map, interview prep, recommendations, CSV/SQL export, and conversation feedback
- Company packets for JPMorgan Chase, Citi, Capital One, Toyota, AT&T
- Conversation playbooks: recruiter, hiring manager, peer, alumni, sponsorship disclosure, follow-up messages
- Interview packets for 6 role families: business systems analyst, data analyst, cybersecurity, cloud security, AI automation, technical project coordinator

**Companion Case Studies:**
- AI Agent Risk Scoring — 8-category risk taxonomy with 0–100 production readiness rubric
- Secure Cloud Evidence Lab — synthetic IAM, SIEM, and GRC artifacts for audit readiness

**Skills Demonstrated:** Python data engineering, SQL analytics, explainable scoring, business analysis, risk-aware design, stakeholder communication, systems thinking

**Tech:** Python, pandas, Streamlit, SQLite, pytest — deliberately no LLM API dependency for portfolio clarity

**Profile:** Enterprise Technology Analyst — AI Automation, Cloud Security & Data Analytics
