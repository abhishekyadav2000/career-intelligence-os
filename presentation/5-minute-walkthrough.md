# 5-Minute Walkthrough — Career Intelligence OS

**Presenter:** Abhishek Yadav  
**Audience:** Recruiter, hiring manager, or portfolio reviewer  
**Run before presenting:** `streamlit run dashboard/app.py`

---

## Minute 0:00–0:30 — Hook + Problem

> "Enterprise technology hiring in DFW spans 50+ employers and hundreds of role families. Most candidates react — apply everywhere, hope for callbacks. I built a decision system that prioritizes, scores, and prepares — turning job search into a measurable process."

**Show:** Title screen / GitHub repo

---

## Minute 0:30–1:30 — Overview Tab

> "This is Career Intelligence OS. Fifty companies, 117 jobs, 75 contacts — all ingested from structured CSVs with schema validation."

**Show:** Overview tab — KPI cards, universal profile, gap matrix P0, fit distribution chart

> "My universal profile is Enterprise Technology Analyst — AI Automation, Cloud Security & Data Analytics. The gap matrix shows what I'm building next — AI risk scoring and cloud security evidence labs."

---

## Minute 1:30–2:30 — Company Ranking + Role Fit

**Show:** Company Ranking tab — top 5 (JPMorgan, Goldman, Citi)

> "Companies are ranked by composite priority — job count, contacts, fit scores, sponsorship signal. This isn't random targeting."

**Show:** Role Fit tab — drill into one high-fit role, show six-category breakdown

> "Every role gets an explainable score across six categories — technical fit, business fit, sponsorship signal, DFW relevance, networking opportunity, and noise risk. You can see exactly WHY a role scores high."

---

## Minute 2:30–3:30 — Sponsorship + Networking

**Show:** Sponsorship Signal tab

> "Sponsorship signals are indicative only — not legal certainty. Every view includes a disclaimer. I verify via DOL/USCIS for actual applications."

**Show:** Networking Map tab — one outreach angle

> "The system generates outreach angles by contact type — recruiter, hiring manager, peer, alumni. Professional tone, not spam."

---

## Minute 3:30–4:15 — Recommendations + Export

**Show:** Recommendations tab — filter to "apply now"

> "Four actions: apply now, network first, research more, skip. Each with rationale. This is decision automation, not a job board."

**Show:** Export tab — SQL demo query, run it

> "CSV export plus SQLite analytics with joins and CTEs. Data engineering, not just dashboard pretty pictures."

---

## Minute 4:15–4:45 — Conversation Feedback Loop

**Show:** Conversation Feedback tab

> "This is what makes it an operating system, not a one-time tool. I log every outreach and interview conversation. The analyzer detects warm companies, repeated objections, skill gaps, and next actions. Interview insights feed back into portfolio improvements."

---

## Minute 4:45–5:00 — Close

> "Career Intelligence OS is my proof triangle — working demo, business case, and conversation weapon. Companion case studies cover AI agent risk scoring and cloud security evidence collection. The repo is public, the dashboard runs locally, and I'm happy to go deeper on any module."
