## Recording Tips

- Record at 1440×900 or use the dashboard locally at `localhost:8501`
- Pre-select JPMorgan Chase + AI Automation Analyst before recording
- Generate the brief once before recording so it's instant on camera
- Keep mouse movement slow — pause on each tab for 2–3 seconds
- Optional: split into two Looms (60s overview + 60s ICC deep dive) if 90s feels rushed
