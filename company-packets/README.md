# Company Packets

Pre-researched company briefs for targeted outreach and interview preparation. Each packet combines **repo sample data** with **general public information** — no live scraping, no proprietary data.

## How to Use

1. Pick a target company from your ranked list (Company Ranking tab)
2. Open the corresponding packet below
3. Review talking points, role matches, and outreach angles
4. Log the conversation in `data/conversation_log_template.csv`
5. Check Conversation Feedback tab for warm signals and next actions

## Data Sources

- `data/sample_companies.csv` — company metadata, sponsor signals, target roles
- `data/sample_jobs.csv` — role descriptions and fit scores
- `data/sample_contacts.csv` — contact types and outreach targets
- Public career pages and general employer information (cited in each packet)

## Disclaimer

Sponsorship signals are indicative only. Verify via DOL/USCIS for each role. Company information reflects public sources and sample data — not insider knowledge.
