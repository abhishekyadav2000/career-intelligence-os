# Interview Packet: Business Systems Analyst

**Role Family:** Business Systems Analyst  
**Universal Profile Fit:** Enterprise Technology Analyst — AI Automation, Cloud Security & Data Analytics  
**Typical Employers:** JPMorgan Chase, Goldman Sachs, Bank of America, Citi, Charles Schwab, Capital One

---

## Role Summary

Business systems analysts bridge business stakeholders and technology teams — gathering requirements, documenting processes, configuring systems, and ensuring technology solutions meet business needs. In enterprise finance-tech, this often involves Agile delivery, Jira workflows, risk controls, and data-driven reporting.

## Technical Questions (Prepare Answers)

1. **"How do you gather requirements from stakeholders?"**
   → Structured intake: business problem → current state → desired state → acceptance criteria. Reference CI OS business requirements doc as example.

2. **"Describe a time you translated a business need into a technical solution."**
   → CI OS: business problem (fragmented job search) → requirements → modular Python pipeline → Streamlit dashboard.

3. **"How do you prioritize competing requests?"**
   → Recommendation engine: four-action taxonomy (apply/network/research/skip) based on weighted scoring — same logic applies to feature prioritization.

4. **"What's your experience with SQL?"**
   → SQLite demo with joins, aggregations, CTEs in Export tab. Walk through one query live.

5. **"How do you handle ambiguous requirements?"**
   → Gap matrix approach: identify what's known (P0 gaps) vs. needs research. Recommend "research more" action when data is insufficient.
